Space For Giants

I spend an increasing part of my time in Kenya, a country I am proud to call my second home and I would like to encourage visitors to my site to follow the links below, join the March For Giants and make a donation.

I first came across Space For Giants in the UK's Independent Newspaper Christmas campaign some years ago, before I met my Kenyan wife. Having been on a number of safaris, I am priveleged to have seen all of the "Big 5" in their natural habitat, including the mighty Elephant. Space For Giants is working hard to conserve these endangered animals and their habitat, so that future generations may live alongside them.
