Space For Giants

I spend an increasing part of my time in Kenya, a country I am proud to call my second home and I would like to encourage visitors to my site to follow a link below to a charity close to my heart.

I first came across Space For Giants in the Independent Newspaper Christmas campaign some years ago, before I met my Kenyan partner. I have now seen all of the "Big 5" except an Elephant, this charity is working hard so that I may one day see one of these magnificent beasts.
